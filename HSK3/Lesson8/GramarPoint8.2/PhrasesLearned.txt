When you want to express

Whatever, whoever, whenever in chinese

The expression could be

Q1 就 + Q2

1- 想吃什么就吃什么
2- 什么东西便宜就买什么
3- 什么时候方便就什么时候去


If you have different subjects, you can have
these subjects inserted before the first
and the second question.

S1 + Q1 + S2 + 就 + Q2

1- 你坐哪儿我就坐哪儿
2- 你喝什么我就喝什么
3- 你去哪儿我就去哪儿
4- 谁喜欢他，他就喜欢谁。
